Who Are We?

At Emanuel we love, worship & serve in Christ's name. We rejoice in your visit here and hope you find something about this great church that nourishes you, as it provides the strength and encouragement to become all that God created you to be. We want you to know that whoever you are and wherever you are on life's journey, you are welcome here. We hope you will take time to explore who we are on these pages, and then visit us in person - for worship, an event, or for any of the programs we offer. We truly desire to love and serve you in Christ's name.
